Awards & Honors

Recognition earned through years of dedication, discipline, and contribution to Hindustani classical music.

Major Recognitions

  • Conferred the status of ‘A-Grade’ Artist by All India Radio and Doordarshan.
  • Recipient of the prestigious ‘Sur-Mani’ Award by The Sur Singar Samsad. Following his performance, The Times of India described him as a “Find of the Sammelan” and a “Rising Maestro.” This award was presented in 1981 while he was still a college student.
  • Awarded the degree of ‘Sangeet Alankar’ by The Gandharva Mahavidyalaya Mandal, Mumbai, securing the first rank in the instrumental category.
  • Honoured as an Honorary Artist of SPIC-MACAY since its inception, contributing to the promotion of Indian classical music among the youth.
  • Recipient of the ‘Yuva Kalakar’ Award from Sangeetonmesh Sanstha, Pune, presented by Pt. Bhimsen Joshi.
  • Honoured with the ‘Late Pt. B. S. Upadhye Puraskar’, Pune, in the year 2002.
