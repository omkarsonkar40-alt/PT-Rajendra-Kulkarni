Pt. Rajendra Kulkarni

Flute Maestro · Hindustani Classical Music

An outstanding Bansuri maestro renowned for a self-developed and distinctive approach to Hindustani classical instrumental music. His artistry reflects decades of dedicated practice, deep contemplation, and an unwavering commitment to the flute as a complete and expressive musical medium. He is also a good singer.

Flute as the Core Expression

The Bansuri stands at the very heart of his musical journey. His playing embodies tonal purity, melodic clarity, and a deeply meditative quality shaped through years of disciplined riyaaz and performance.

A Distinct Musical Path

Through independent thinking and long-term artistic exploration, he has evolved a personal style that remains rooted in tradition while expressing an unmistakable individual musical voice.

Performer, Guru & Guide

Revered as a flautist and respected as a teacher, he is known among disciples for his openness, humility, and willingness to guide seekers of classical music with patience and clarity.

“The flute is complete in itself — it speaks, sings, and meditates.” — Pt. Rajendra Kulkarni
